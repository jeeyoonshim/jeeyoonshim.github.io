I studied Graphic Design at Maryland Institute College of Art. My interest in code was pipqued with taking a class in Generative Typography in which we used Processing, a coding language, to generate and create art. I was fascinated.

Fast forward to 2023, and I began pursuing programming as a career change, learning Fullstack using the MERN stack. I applied my knowledge of design systems and UX/UI and incorporated that with programming. Things that I've learned before came in handy, including loops and objects, while I learned new skills with the backend side of things — like making a server, a database, and accessing all those routes.

I'm very good at taking concepts and breaking them down so it's easier to explain to professionals from other disciplines. I'd like to credit that to my Graphic Design/Art History background. I'm a fast learner, adaptable and flexible and excel at working in teams.
